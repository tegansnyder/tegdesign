The purpose of this extension is to provide a fully integrated solution for collecting and soliciting email addresses from visitors to your Magento store. This extension provides you with a turn-ley self hosted solution to integrating a popup email collector into your ecommerce store. Notable features include dynamic coupon code creation, Mailchimp synch and autoresponder support, exportable email address grid, and conversion tracking with either Google Analytics or Mixpanel supported out of the box.
